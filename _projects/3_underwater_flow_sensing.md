**Center for Robotics Research, University of Cincinnati · 09/2024 – 05/2025**

Capstone project with Prof. Janet Dong:

- Conducted systematic aquatic propulsion experiments across a three-round parameter optimization campaign.
- Developed an "Underwater Skin" sensor leveraging the deformation-sensing properties of GelSight to measure and visualize flow direction and intensity with high sensitivity.
- Implemented a camera system using RGB lighting to enhance visualization of flow-induced deformations.

This sensing system supports the variable-stiffness propulsion module of an aerial-aquatic locomotion robot {% cite hu2025aerialaquatic %}.
