**REHAssist Group (BioRobotics Laboratory), EPFL · 01/2024 – 09/2024**

Research assistant to Dr. Mohamed Bouri on accurate 2D and 3D guidewire perception for the T-REX endovascular robot:

- **[2D]** Designed a sim-to-real domain adaptation framework that generalizes a prompt-free vision foundation model (SAM) to real-world X-ray fluoroscopy without annotation supervision, achieving 79.35 F1 — close to the supervised upper bound of 80.53 F1 {% cite wen2024generalizing %}.
- **[2D]** Designed a semi-supervised medical image segmentation network achieving state-of-the-art performance across modalities and targets, including left atrial (MRI), pancreas (MDCT), and brain tumor (FLAIR MRI) {% cite wen2025expanding %}.
- **[3D]** Designed PASTE (PArametric STructural Encoding), an end-to-end learning-based guidewire reconstruction method handling arbitrary guidewire shapes via a novel octa-plane representation, achieving submillimeter accuracy (0.55 mm ± 0.17 mm mean error) on the Neurovascular dataset {% cite paste2025 %}.
- Investigated factors influencing robotic mechanical thrombectomy and developed strategies for optimizing clot extraction.

First author on three papers from this project {% cite paste2025 %} {% cite wen2024generalizing %} {% cite wen2025expanding %}.
