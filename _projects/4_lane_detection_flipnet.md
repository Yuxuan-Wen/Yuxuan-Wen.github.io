**Intelligent Networks and Advanced Computing Lab, Chongqing University · 12/2021 – 07/2022**
*(National Natural Science Foundation of China: 61962038)*

Designed and implemented FlipNet, a novel segmentation-based method that improves lane detection under severe occlusion, discontinuous lane appearance, and illumination variation.

- Evaluated on three major lane detection datasets: CULane, TuSimple, and LLAMAS.
- Achieved the new best score among segmentation-based methods, running at 59 FPS (1.65× faster than prior work).
- First author, published in IEEE Transactions on Intelligent Transportation Systems {% cite wen2024flipnet %}.
