**t.Dunn Lab, Duke University · 09/2025 – Current**

Currently building a foundation model of lab animal behavior capable of multiple downstream tasks:

- Animal model identification
- Action recognition
- Neural decoding

This project is part of my doctoral research with Prof. Timothy W. Dunn, and is connected to the TULIP-Bench dataset work on multi-perspective video and 3D pose capture for Parkinson's disease assessments {% cite tulipbench2026 %} {% cite explainable3dpd2026 %}.
