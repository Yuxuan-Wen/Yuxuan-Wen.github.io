**Intelligent Networks and Advanced Computing Lab, Chongqing University · 07/2022 – 12/2023**
*(National Natural Science Foundation of China: 62262045, 61962038)*

- Analyzed the discrepant attention-shifting dynamics between the two fundamental tasks in object detection (classification and localization), exploring deeper head structures to boost performance.
- Designed methods that leverage this task conflict to guide optimal model design; validated generalizability across architectures, label assignment methods, training techniques, and datasets.
- First author on two papers in top journals: IEEE TNNLS {% cite wen2025bridging %} and JMLR (under review) {% cite wen2025scalabledepth %}.
